1- the company and the employees got hit by a phishing attack through an email that pretends to be the manager this attack is called spear phishing,it targets specific type of users and it pretends that it originate from a trusted source which it would lead after to a ransomware which can encrypt the company data and files

2- first step to do is isolate the affected system to contain the infection and prevent the ransomware from spreading infected systems should be remove from the network immediately and secure backups. if the backups are infected and there is no decryption tool available the org should consider paying the ransom

3-Patch the exploited vulnerabilityr emove malicious artifacts files, scheduled tasks, registry entries, rogue accounts, persistence mechanisms and restore systems from clean, verified backups (or rebuild from known-good images) validate system integrity before reconnecting to the network Gradually bring systems back online, monitoring closely for signs of reinfection restore data and confirm integrity (checksums, application testing)

4-post incident review and team meeting is a must after a huge blow to the company while breaking down what happend during the attack and what can be fixed in the future while improving detection systems and launch a training program to avoid phishing attacks and fix the gaps
